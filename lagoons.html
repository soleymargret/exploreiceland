LAGOONS YOU CAN TAKE A BATH IN

The Blue Lagoon

The famous Blue Lagoon, one of the 25 wonders of the world by Natural Geographic, is known for its milky-blue waters surrounded by lava fields. It's located just a short drive from Reykjavik, this geothermal spa is rich in minerals like silica and sulfur, which are said to benefit the skin.

Learn more about the Blue Lagoon

The Sky Lagoon

A modern geothermal spa located just minutes from Reykjavik's city center. Opening in 2021, this oceanside lagoon offers a oceanside 250 feet infinity pool with a swim-up bar and a seven-step ritual you can savor. The spa's design draws inspiration from Icelandic nature and heritage.

Learn more about the Sky Lagoon

Hvammsvik

A hot spring located in Iceland's Whale Fjord 45 minute drive from Reykjavik. This costal retreat features eight natural hot springs of different temperatures that blend right into the rocky coastline. The views are increadible, and if you are feeling brave you can take a dip in the Atlantic Ocean.
