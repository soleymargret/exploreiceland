THINGS TO DO IN VESTMANNAEYJAR

RIB boat tour

This Ribsafari boat tour is a great way to see the puffins, rock formations, wildflife and the amazing Westman Islands. I will be able to go into a cave where the tour guide plays his saxaphone and it sounds amazing inside the cave.

Visit their page

Sea life sanctuary

This is a great sanctuary where you can see their beluga whales that are very freindly. It is also Iceland's only Puffin Rescue Centre as well as other local aquarium species.

https://belugasanctuary.sealifetrust.org/en/about-us/the-sanctuary/

Visit their page

Gott reasturant

Gott is a great healthy family restaurant in the Westman Islands that uses only fresh and healthy ingrediants. Every sauce, stew, soup, and cake are prepared from scratch at the restaurant and the fish is locally bought.
