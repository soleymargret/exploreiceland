THE GOLDEN CIRCLE

Gullfoss

The beautiful waterfall called Gullfoss, meaning "Golden Falls" is a two-tiered waterfall with the total drop of 110 feet. It's a short little hike to it, with trails leading to different viewpoints. You can get as close to it so you feel the mist of the water.

Geysir

A beautiful Geyser hot spring in a geothermal area, with a the one big Geysir and then a few other neighboring geysers as well as hot spring pools with bright blue water. It's the place that gave all geysers their name. It's a great spot to experience Iceland's increadible geothermal activity up close.


Thingvellir

The countries national park, full of natural beauty and historical significance. Iceland's first parlament was founded there over 1,000 years ago, making it one of the oldest parlaments in the world. The park also sits right between two techtonic plates, the North American and Eurasian which are slowly drifting apart creating cracks and cliffs in the landscape.
